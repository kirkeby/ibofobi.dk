Skewed

Skewed is yet another framework/library/thingy for writing web-applications in Python, it is built on top of WSGI, Paste and Entwine. I have not yet written any documentation or examples, but I will get around to it.

Get it

For now you can only get it with subversion; first you will want to get Paste and Entwine, then you can check it out from svn://ibofobi.dk/public/trunk/skewed.
